A LITTLE ABOUT ME

My name is Xinxin He. I got a bachelor in software engineering at Beihang University, and a master degree in Human-Computer Interaction at Indiana University - Purdue University, Indianapolis. I'm working as a System Analyst at Telamon Corporation right now, designing business process solutions for our clients.


I am a sketch-driven designer. Sketching out whatever ideas, scenarios, workflows, modules, relations at first can help me get a great overview of what I am going to head to and. Furthermore, sketching is also an efficient and enjoyable skill to facilitate communications with other people. Besides that, I also love the process of converting user requirements into working demos,iterating and refining them until "Perfect".
